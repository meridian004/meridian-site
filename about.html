About Meridian Strategic Resources

Meridian Strategic Resources, LLC is a Riverside, California–based firm dedicated to enabling organisations to thrive in today’s complex procurement landscape. As a minority‑owned company, we comply fully with federal and state regulations while delivering precise procurement strategies, resilient infrastructure and innovative ESG solutions that empower our clients in both government and commercial markets.

Our Story

California native Wes Mitchell founded Meridian Strategic Resources out of a passion for sustainable growth and public‑sector innovation. Raised along the Golden State’s coastline, he has always been committed to preserving natural resources and strengthening communities. After decades leading federal contracting and supply chain initiatives, he launched MSR to deliver eco‑conscious procurement and infrastructure solutions that help governments and businesses thrive while protecting the planet for future generations.

Values

  • Integrity – conducting business with honesty, ethics and accountability.
  • Innovation – leveraging technology and fresh thinking to deliver better outcomes.
  • Resilience – building systems and strategies that withstand the toughest challenges.
  • Inclusivity – embracing diversity and championing equitable opportunities.
