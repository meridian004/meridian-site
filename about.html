About Meridian Strategic Resources

Meridian Strategic Resources, LLC is a Riverside, California based firm dedicated to enabling organisations to thrive in today’s complex procurement landscape. We are certified as minority‑owned and fully compliant with federal and state regulations. Our mission is to deliver precision procurement strategies, resilient infrastructure and innovative ESG solutions that enable our clients to outperform in government and commercial markets.

Our Story

Founded by Wes Mathews, Meridian Strategic Resources grew out of a desire to provide high‑trust, reliable services to public and private sector clients. We understand the critical importance of compliance, transparency and performance. That’s why our team draws on decades of experience in federal contracting, supply chain management and digital transformation to deliver unmatched results.

Values

  • Integrity – conducting business with honesty, ethics and accountability.
  • Innovation – leveraging technology and fresh thinking to deliver better outcomes.
  • Resilience – building systems and strategies that withstand the toughest challenges.
  • Inclusivity – embracing diversity and championing equitable opportunities.
