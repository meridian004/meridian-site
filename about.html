About Us

Meridian Strategic Resources is a veteran‑owned enterprise dedicated to enabling efficient procurement and logistics for federal and private organizations.

Our Mission

We exist to bridge the gap between government procurement and private sector innovation. Our mission is to deliver credible, compliant and sustainable solutions that uplift communities and elevate our clients' mission readiness.

Founder’s Manifesto

Wesley Mitchell, founder and principal, built MSR after witnessing first‑hand the inefficiencies in federal contracting. His manifesto is simple: integrity, technical precision, ESG alignment, sovereignty and equity. These principles guide every decision we make.
