Our Story

California native Wes Mitchell founded Meridian Strategic Resources to build data systems that help governments design resilient infrastructure. Inspired by Riverside’s rich history and the urgent need for environmental stewardship, Wes partnered with data scientists, engineers, and community advocates to create a firm dedicated to sustainable development and inclusive growth.

Today, our team continues to champion intelligent, transparent, and equitable infrastructure solutions that empower communities across California and beyond.
